## The Features That Actually Matter

Let's talk about what this thing can actually do, beyond just "you type, they type back."

**Real-Time Visitor Monitoring**

You can see who's on your website right now, what pages they're viewing, where they came from, their location, browser type, and how long they've been browsing. This isn't creepy surveillance - it's context. When someone starts a chat, you already know they've been stuck on your pricing page for five minutes, so you can proactively offer help.

**Proactive Chat Invitations**

Speaking of which, you can set up automatic chat invitations based on visitor behavior. Someone's been on your checkout page for 30 seconds? Pop up a friendly "Need any help with your order?" message. It's subtle, but it works.

**Canned Responses**

For the questions you get asked fifty times a day ("What's your return policy?" "Do you ship internationally?"), you can create pre-written responses. Just click, send, and move on to the next customer. Saves a ton of time.

**Agent-to-Agent Chat**

Your team members can chat with each other internally without the customer seeing. Useful for "Hey, do we have this item in stock?" type questions without making the customer wait while you check.

**Chat Transfer**

If a customer needs to talk to someone else on your team - maybe they need technical support instead of sales - you can seamlessly transfer the conversation to another agent, complete with the full chat history.

**File Transfer**

Customers can send you files directly through the chat window. Screenshots of error messages, photos of damaged products, whatever they need to show you. Makes troubleshooting way easier.

**Multi-Language Support**

The chat window supports 41 languages. The operator console apps support English, German, Chinese, French, and Russian. If you're serving a global audience, this matters.

**Mobile Apps**

Your agents don't need to be chained to a desk. There are apps for both iOS and Android, so you can answer chats from your phone if needed.

**Integration Options**

Works with WordPress, Shopify, Magento, Wix, Joomla, and pretty much any platform where you can paste a snippet of HTML. The setup takes maybe five minutes.
